# Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 17, 2026

## Overview

My Helper is a Chrome extension that teaches people how to use ChatGPT and Codex with page guidance, prompt coaching, language options, voice support, and accessibility tools.

## Information kept on your device

My Helper stores language preferences, voice preferences, accessibility settings, learning progress, achievements, and the assistant position in Chrome extension storage on your device.

## Google account

My Helper requires a Google sign-in before coaching features can be used. It receives the basic account information needed to identify your account, such as your email address and display name. Supabase synchronizes selected preferences and learning progress to your My Helper account.

## Support messages

Support is available only after you sign in. When you choose to send a support message, My Helper stores the category you select, the message you write, and the account email connected to the request so an authorized support operator can investigate and reply. A secure server-side function may send the ticket to the My Helper support inbox and send you an automatic confirmation. My Helper never automatically sends your ChatGPT conversations, chat history, uploaded files, screenshots, or unsent drafts as part of a support request.

## Information sent for AI features

My Helper sends information only when you choose an AI feature. When you select Coach this prompt, the prompt you selected is sent to the My Helper coach server. When you ask a question, request a guided lesson, or use Explain this page, My Helper may send your question, current page context, and labels of visible controls.

My Helper does not automatically send ChatGPT conversations, chat history, uploaded files, account settings, or messages you have not chosen to coach.

## Visual analysis

Visual analysis is optional. When you enable it and request an explanation, My Helper may capture a temporary image of the visible browser tab. The image is used only for that request and is deleted after use or within thirty seconds. It is not saved as part of your learning profile.

## Voice

My Helper may use voices installed on your device. When cloud voice is configured and you request it, the text to be spoken may be sent to OpenAI to generate audio. Voice questions may be sent to the My Helper coach server only after you choose to speak or submit a voice request.

## Service providers

My Helper uses OpenRouter for requested AI coaching, explanations, translations, and guided learning. It may use OpenAI for requested cloud speech generation, Supabase for authentication and synchronization, and a transactional email provider for support messages you choose to submit.

## Data sharing and retention

My Helper does not sell personal data or use it for advertising. Local preferences remain until you remove the extension or clear its extension data. Synchronized account information, progress, and support messages remain until you delete your My Helper account data or the information is no longer needed to operate the service. Support messages are rate-limited to help protect the service from abuse.

## Your choices

You can manage preferences in My Helper settings, remove the extension, clear extension storage, or delete synchronized My Helper account data after signing in. Deleting My Helper account data does not delete your Google account or data held by other services.
