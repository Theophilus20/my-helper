# Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 17, 2026

## Overview

My Helper is a Chrome extension that teaches people how to use ChatGPT and Codex with page guidance, prompt coaching, language options, voice support, and accessibility tools.

## Information Stored on Your Device

My Helper stores language preferences, voice preferences, accessibility settings, learning progress, achievements, and assistant position in Chrome extension storage on your device.

## Information Sent for AI Features

My Helper sends information only when you choose an AI feature.

When you select Coach this prompt, the prompt you selected is sent to the My Helper coach server for feedback.

When you ask a question, request a guided lesson, or use Explain this page, My Helper may send your question, the current page context, and labels of visible controls to the coach server.

My Helper does not automatically send ChatGPT conversations, chat history, uploaded files, account settings, or messages you have not chosen to coach.

## Visual Analysis

Visual analysis is optional.

When you enable visual analysis and request an explanation, My Helper may capture a temporary image of the visible browser tab. A visible tab image can include anything currently displayed on the screen, including chat content.

The image is used only to answer that request. It is deleted after use or within thirty seconds. My Helper uses the live page map to choose the final highlighted control.

## Voice

My Helper may use voices installed on your computer.

When a matching local voice is unavailable and cloud voice is configured, text may be sent to OpenAI to generate speech audio.

Voice questions may be sent to the My Helper coach server only after you speak or submit a voice request.

## Service Providers

My Helper uses OpenRouter for AI coaching, explanations, translations, and guided learning.

My Helper may use OpenAI for cloud speech generation when that option is configured.

## Data Sharing

My Helper does not sell personal data.

My Helper does not use personal data for advertising.

## Data Retention

My Helper does not store ChatGPT conversations, screenshots, or prompts by default.

Local preferences and learning progress remain in Chrome extension storage until you remove the extension or clear its stored data.

## Contact

For privacy or security concerns, use the private reporting option in this repository.
