Privacy Policy

This policy explains how My Helper handles information when you use the Chrome extension and its optional account features.

Information kept on your device

My Helper stores your language, voice, accessibility, learning-progress, achievement, and assistant-position preferences in Chrome extension storage on your device.

Google account

My Helper requires a Google sign-in before coaching features can be used. It receives the basic account information needed to identify your account, such as your email address and display name. Supabase synchronizes selected preferences and learning progress to your My Helper account.

Support messages

Support is available only after you sign in. When you choose to send a support message, My Helper stores the category you select, the message you write, and the account email connected to the request so an authorized support operator can investigate and reply. A secure server-side function may send the ticket to the My Helper support inbox and send you an automatic confirmation. My Helper never automatically sends your ChatGPT conversations, chat history, uploaded files, screenshots, or unsent drafts as part of a support request.

Information sent for coaching

My Helper sends information only when you ask it to coach a prompt, answer a question, give a lesson, explain a page, or create voice guidance. Depending on the feature, that can include the text you submit, your question, the current page context, and labels of visible controls. My Helper does not automatically send ChatGPT conversations, chat history, uploaded files, account settings, or unsent messages you have not chosen to coach.

Optional visual analysis

If you enable visual analysis and request a page explanation, My Helper may capture a temporary image of the visible browser tab. A screenshot can include anything visible on that tab. It is used only for that request, is deleted after use or within thirty seconds, and is not saved as part of your learning profile.

Voice

My Helper may use voices installed on your device. When cloud voice is configured and you request it, the text to be spoken may be sent to OpenAI to generate audio. Voice questions are sent only after you choose to speak or submit a voice request.

Service providers

My Helper uses OpenRouter for requested AI coaching, explanations, translations, and guided learning. It may use OpenAI for requested cloud speech generation, Supabase for authentication and synchronization, and a transactional email provider for support messages you choose to submit.

Sharing, advertising, and retention

My Helper does not sell personal data or use it for advertising. Local preferences remain on your device until you remove the extension or clear its extension data. Synchronized account information, progress, and support messages remain until you delete your My Helper account data or the information is no longer needed to operate the service. Support messages are rate-limited to help protect the service from abuse.

Your choices

You can manage preferences in My Helper settings, remove the extension, clear extension storage, or delete synchronized My Helper account data after signing in. Deleting My Helper account data does not delete your Google account or data held by other services.
